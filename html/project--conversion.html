About the company Rentalcars.com

About the company Rentalcars.com

North Star

  • When I started in 2015 Rentalcars had a very clear single KPI, increasing BPD, bookings per day, more conversion. The A/B test platform provided the data on that helped to decide weather a change on the platform was making more money. The platform was able to determine very precisely which increase in sales an experiment will generate.
  • In the screenshot you can still see the urgency messages which were the results of a couple of very successful experiments.

About the company Rentalcars.com

Regular ideation session

  • Crossfunctional teams with develpers, designers and product managers were responsible for a specific part of the user journey. Our team took care of the 'search results' and 'car details' pages. To fill our backlog every team organised weekly ideation sessions. Most experiments only allowed very small changes like the copy 'Unlimited mileage' I've added in the screenshot.
  • One source for my ideas have been vision prototypes I've tested with colleagues and customers.

Topic 02

UI Design studies

  • As mentioned a purly A/B testing driven design development only allowes very small changes. So you would not expect that UI vision prototyping can help here much. On the screenshot you can see a flow where I experiemented with different gray tones to focus and highight on important elements of the UI.
  • Making the background colour for all pages more dark was a gold experiment on the A/B test platform.
